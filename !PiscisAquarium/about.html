Our Story

Our fish shop has been opened since 1990. at the beginning we just a small fish shop on the street. We start having so many marine animals fans. slowly our store became big enough, as for today we have been opened for 32 years. We have been through a lot of trouble in our journey. we have experienced where we have zero customer for 3 months. but we didn't stop to introduce people to marine animals. we start promoting marine animals and share about interesting facts about every each different marine animals. We grow and bred our fish, we want to make sure we didn't harm the fish. Now we are here in the online forms we hope our customer can choose their dream marine animals here.
